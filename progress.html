Design of Cross Section

 Featuring a well-designed four-lane carriageway, with
each lane measuring a generous 3.6 meters in width.
Enhancing safety and convenience, the road includes
0.75-meter inner shoulders and spacious 2.5-meter
outer shoulders. The right of way spans between 30 to
50 meters, ensuring ample space for smooth and efficient
traffic flow. This thoughtful design aims to provide
a comfortable and secure driving experience for all
road users.
